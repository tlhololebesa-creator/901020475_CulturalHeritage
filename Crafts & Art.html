Crafts & Art


Basotho crafts are part of the Basotho heritage. The Basotho craft can include: Mokorotlo, Basotho blanket, Weaving, Beadwork and Pottery.


Mokorotlo: This are woven grass hats, which are considered the symbol of the Basotho culture.

Basotho blankets: This are blankets made from weaves and they also include vibrant and eyecatching colours.

Weaving: This a process of creating items such as baskests, mats and other items using grass and mohair.

Beadwork: This is the making of traditional ornaments, which often shows the symbol of the culture.

Pottery: It is an artistic way of creating traditional ornaments.
